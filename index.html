Cars MPG Observation by Fuel and Cylinder

This is an interactive narrative visualization using D3.js to analyze automobile data from 2017. It features different scenes, including a scatter plot and a bar chart, to compare metrics like city and highway MPG, visually representing these comparisons and trends through user-driven interactions. The visualization is enhanced with dynamic elements such as tooltips and sorting functionalities, allowing users to explore in-depth data based on vehicle attributes like engine cylinders and fuel type.

The scatterplot chart illustrates the relationship between Average Highway MPG (X-axis) and Average City MPG (Y-axis), using circles to represent data points where the size indicates the number of Engine Cylinders. It shows a positive correlation, indicating that vehicles with higher city MPG often also achieve higher highway MPG. The data points cluster in patterns that reflect distinct groups by cylinder count.

Car MPG Narrative Visualization by Hilda Liu.
